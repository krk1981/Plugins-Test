# M-Market Finland Digitization Project Plan
## Digital Transformation Implementation

**Project Start:** Q2 2026
**Project Duration:** 12 months
**Project Manager:** [To be assigned]
**Client:** M-Market Finland (53 stores)

---

## Project Overview

### Objective
Transform M-Market's 53 independent grocery stores through comprehensive digital modernization, enabling competitive capabilities while preserving independent retailer autonomy and community focus.

### Success Criteria
- **Revenue Growth:** 15% increase in participating stores within 12 months
- **Digital Adoption:** 40% customer engagement with digital services
- **Operational Efficiency:** 20% reduction in manual processes
- **System Integration:** Seamless operation with Wihuri logistics partnership

### Project Scope

**In Scope:**
- E-commerce platform and mobile application development
- POS system integration and upgrade
- Inventory management and AI optimization systems
- Customer loyalty and personalization platform
- Staff training and change management
- Integration with Wihuri logistics systems

**Out of Scope:**
- Physical store renovations or equipment replacement
- Marketing campaign development and execution
- Financial system integration beyond POS
- Third-party supplier system modifications

## Risk Management

### High-Risk Items
1. **Wihuri Integration Complexity**
   - **Mitigation:** Early technical assessment and dedicated integration specialist

2. **Store Manager Adoption Resistance**
   - **Mitigation:** Comprehensive change management and incentive programs

3. **Customer Digital Adoption Rate**
   - **Mitigation:** User-friendly design and gradual feature introduction

4. **Technical Performance Issues**
   - **Mitigation:** Extensive testing and gradual rollout approach

### Medium-Risk Items
1. **Budget Overruns**
   - **Mitigation:** Regular budget reviews and scope management

2. **Timeline Delays**
   - **Mitigation:** Agile development approach and buffer time allocation

3. **Staff Turnover During Implementation**
   - **Mitigation:** Knowledge documentation and cross-training
