## Team Assignments

### Account Team Responsibilities

**Account Executive**
- Own executive relationship and deal progression
- Coordinate competitive strategy and pricing
- Manage proposal process and contract negotiations

**Solution Architect**
- Lead technical discovery and requirements gathering
- Design integration architecture and implementation approach
- Support technical evaluation and proof-of-concept activities

**Project Manager**
- Develop detailed implementation timeline and resource plan
- Coordinate internal team and vendor management
- Own delivery risk assessment and mitigation planning

### Escalation Contacts
- **Sales Director**: For pricing authority and competitive strategy
- **Delivery Director**: For resource allocation and timeline commits
- **Technical Director**: For complex integration and architecture decisions

## Risk Monitoring & Alerts

### Automated Monitoring
- **Page Activity**: Alert if no updates for >1 week during active deal phases
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Flag if >2 weeks since last customer interaction
- **Competitive Activity**: Monitor for competitor mentions in deal communications
- **Timeline Adherence**: Alert if milestones are at risk based on current progress

### Manual Review Triggers
- **Scope Changes**: Major modifications to project requirements or timeline
- **Budget Adjustments**: Material changes to investment level or financing approach
- **Organizational Changes**: New stakeholders or changes in decision-making authority
- **Competitive Threats**: New vendors entering evaluation or pricing pressure

## Post-Deal Transition

### Success Scenario: Deal Won
- **Project Handoff**: Transfer Confluence workspace to delivery team
- **Customer Success**: Transition account management to customer success team
- **Reference Development**: Begin case study and reference customer cultivation
- **Expansion Planning**: Identify Phase 2 opportunities and additional use cases

### Learning Scenario: Deal Lost
- **Post-Mortem Analysis**: Comprehensive review of loss factors and lessons learned
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Deep dive on winning vendor and their approach
- **Relationship Maintenance**: Keep door open for future opportunities
- **Process Improvement**: Apply learnings to sales process and methodology refinements
