### What They Do
M-Market operates as a cooperative of independent grocery retailers across Finland, serving local communities with neighborhood grocery stores. They were founded by retailers who opposed consolidation and maintain independence through shared logistics and supplier agreements.

### Recent Context
- **Independent Positioning** — 2006-Present — Founded by retailers opposing SOK acquisition, values independence
- **Logistics Partnership** — 2018 — Switched to Wihuri Oy from Tuko Logistics for supply chain
- **Supplier Network** — Current — Maintains agreements with ~100 partners for product sourcing

### Market Challenges
- **Digital Competition** — Large chains (K-Group, S-Group) investing heavily in digital platforms
- **Market Pressure** — 0.3% market share suggests need for differentiation and growth
