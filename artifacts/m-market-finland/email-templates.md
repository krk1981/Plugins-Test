## Email Guidelines

### Tone & Style
- Use "Hei" (informal Finnish greeting) to show cultural awareness
- Professional but approachable tone
- Focus on business value, not technical features
- Include specific metrics and benchmarks
- Keep emails concise and scannable

### Key Messages to Reinforce
1. **Competitive urgency:** K-Group and S-Group automation investments
2. **Proven results:** Specific metrics from similar retailers
3. **Low risk approach:** Pilot program minimizes investment risk
4. **Finnish market knowledge:** Understanding of local context
5. **Partnership mindset:** Long-term relationship, not just transaction

### Follow-Up Timing
- Initial email: Follow up in 5-7 business days
- Discovery call: Within 24 hours with summary and next steps
- Proposal: Follow up in 3-5 business days
- Contract: Weekly check-ins during negotiation phase
