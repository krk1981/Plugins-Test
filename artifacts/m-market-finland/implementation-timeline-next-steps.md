## Risk Management & Contingency Plans

### Technical Risks
**Risk**: Integration challenges with legacy POS systems
**Contingency**:
- Budget additional €200K for custom integration development
- Engage specialist integration partners
- Implement manual backup processes during transition

**Risk**: Performance issues during high-traffic periods
**Contingency**:
- Implement auto-scaling cloud infrastructure
- Conduct stress testing before each rollout phase
- Maintain 24/7 technical support during launch periods

### Business Risks
**Risk**: Store owner resistance to digital adoption
**Contingency**:
- Enhanced training program with dedicated support staff
- Financial incentives for early adopters
- Gradual rollout allowing peer influence and success stories

**Risk**: Competitive response from K-Group/S-Group
**Contingency**:
- Accelerate unique feature development
- Focus on local community differentiation
- Implement aggressive pricing strategies for customer acquisition
