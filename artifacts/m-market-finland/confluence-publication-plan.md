## Best Practices for Deal Confluence Spaces

### Content Quality
- **Keep pages scannable** with clear headers and bullet points
- **Use consistent formatting** across all deal pages
- **Include action items** with owners and due dates
- **Link to external resources** rather than duplicating content

### Collaboration
- **@ mention team members** for specific actions or input
- **Use page comments** for discussion and feedback
- **Track changes** for important strategic documents
- **Create task lists** for complex activities with multiple steps

### Organization
- **Use logical page hierarchy** that follows deal progression
- **Create shortcuts** for frequently accessed pages
- **Archive outdated content** but maintain history
- **Cross-reference related opportunities** for pattern recognition
