## Executive Summary

M-Market Finland is a 53-store grocery retail chain with €59.6M annual revenue seeking digitization to compete against larger players like K-Group and S-Group who are investing heavily in automation. The Finnish retail market is prioritizing operational platforms, AI-driven inventory optimization, and fulfillment technology in 2026, making this an opportune time to propose a comprehensive digitization solution.

### Business Overview
M-Market operates 53 grocery stores across Finland, offering groceries, wine, spirits, and specialty products under private label brands including garden products (Biloan) and fresh baked goods (M-Market brand). They compete directly with market leaders K-Group (Kesko) and S-Group in the highly efficiency-driven Finnish retail market.

### Recent Context & Market Position
- Small but established player in competitive Finnish grocery market
- Competing against heavily automated competitors (Kesko, S-Group)
- Operating in market where automation and data-driven planning are becoming standard
- Finnish retail forecast shows growth resuming in 2026, making investment timing strategic
