## Executive Summary

M-Market Finland faces critical competitive pressure from heavily automated major retailers K-Group and S-Group, who are investing tens of millions in AI-powered retail transformation. With only 0.3% market share across 53 stores, M-Market must execute comprehensive digitization to survive and compete effectively.

This proposal outlines a €385K digitization initiative that will transform M-Market from a manual, fragmented operation into a data-driven, automated retail network capable of competing with industry leaders.

**Strategic Imperatives:**

- **Market Expansion:** Grow from 0.3% to 0.5% market share through efficiency gains
