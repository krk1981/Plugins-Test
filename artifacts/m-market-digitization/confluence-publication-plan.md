## Publication Strategy Overview

Create a centralized Confluence workspace for the M-Market digitization initiative that serves as the authoritative source for strategy, research, proposal documentation, and implementation planning. This space will support stakeholder communication, decision-making, and knowledge management throughout the sales and implementation lifecycle.

**Strategic Objectives:**
- Centralize digitization strategy and research findings
- Enable cross-functional collaboration on proposal development
- Track proposal progression and stakeholder engagement
- Maintain institutional knowledge for similar digitization opportunities

## Content Governance & Maintenance

### Document Ownership Matrix
| Content Area | Primary Owner | Secondary Owner | Update Frequency |
|--------------|---------------|----------------|------------------|
| Strategic Overview | Initiative Lead | Strategy Manager | Weekly |
| Market Research | Business Development | Sales Team | Bi-weekly |
| Technical Proposal | Solution Architect | Technical Lead | As needed |
| Sales Materials | Account Executive | Sales Manager | Weekly |
| Implementation Plan | Project Manager | Solution Architect | Bi-weekly |
| Progress Tracking | Initiative Lead | Project Manager | Daily |

### Quality Assurance Process
1. **Content Review Cycle:** Weekly review of all key pages for accuracy and relevance
2. **Stakeholder Validation:** Monthly validation of market research and competitive analysis
3. **Technical Accuracy:** Quarterly review of technical architecture and implementation plans
4. **Strategic Alignment:** Monthly alignment check with company strategic priorities

### Version Control & Archival
- **Active Initiative Phase:** All content maintained and current
- **Proposal Submitted:** Lock core proposal content, maintain only progress tracking
- **Initiative Won:** Archive with implementation transition documentation
- **Initiative Deferred:** Archive with revival strategy and lessons learned
- **Knowledge Transfer:** Extract reusable templates and best practices for future use

## Risk Management & Contingency Planning

### Content Security & Access Control
- **Sensitive Information:** Restrict access to financial projections and competitive intelligence
- **Stakeholder Privacy:** Limit access to stakeholder contact information and interaction notes
- **Intellectual Property:** Protect proprietary technical architecture and implementation methodologies
- **Competitive Data:** Secure storage of competitor analysis and market intelligence

### Backup & Recovery Plans
- **Weekly Content Backups:** Export key pages to backup storage systems
- **Distributed Ownership:** Ensure multiple team members can maintain critical content
- **Alternative Access:** Maintain offline copies of essential proposal materials
- **Disaster Recovery:** Documented process for space reconstruction if needed
